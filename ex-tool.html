Meet The YAK Extension Tool

The YAK Spreader / Extension Tool was developed out of the need for a long and short length adjustable tool that can hold materials firmly in place. The YAK works by applying a large amount of pressure against the materials you are working with so that the material can be fastened or worked on without moving, while also being easily adjusted or moved as a whole to different locations. No other tool offers the security and mobility of the YAK Extension Tool.

The YAK Spreader / Extension Tool is easy to use and incredibly versatile. No other tool offers the security and mobility of the YAK Extension Tool. If you can think it, YAK can do it.

The Versatility of the YAK Extension Tool

YAK takes the limited ability of a bar clamp/spreader and extends it to as long as you need for any job. It's as simple as cutting a 2x2 to the length needed and installing the YAK parts for either clamping or spreading. The Spreading extension system can hold material securely to a wall, ceiling or floor. It can be used for gluing, straightening or securing for installation.

With the attachment for the plastic wall room divider you can quickly section off a part of a room. Simply measure your ceiling height, cut your 2x2's to length, attach your YAK and spreader and cut a 1x4 and plastic of the length you need. Then roll your 1x4 into plastic three times and slide into the forked slot of YAK. Stand YAK Spreader, 1x4 and plastic up, apply tension needed to support room divider, tape off areas needed.

The YAK Clamping extension system can be made long enough and strong enough for all clamping jobs in all trades. Just cut your 2x2 to size and you can clamp anything from a small cabinet frame to the largest door frame.
